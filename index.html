  Biography

My name is Yongfeng Gu, I am now a doctor candidate in State Key Lab of Software Engineering (SKLSE), Wuhan University. Early on, I studied for my Bachelor degree of Computer science and technology in Hubei university from 2011-2015. Then in summer of 2015, I was admitted to Wuhan University to continue my work for Master degree in SKLSE. In 2017, I decided to continue study for Doctor degree in Computer software and theory, which is under the supervisor of Dr. Jifeng Xuan.

My current research direction is Crash Analysis, and I am also insterested in Mining Software Repository and Web Development.

  Research

Crash Analysis

Crash Analysis, which includes the analysis of crash reporting system, crash reproduction, crash localization, bug fixing and related empirical studies, is a vital process in software testing and debugging.

I am familiar with tools related to static analysis, crash reports, and program mutation, such as spoon, mozilla reporting system, pitest, randoop and so on.

Mining Software Repository

Mining Software Repository (MSR) is another interesting research area. Nowadays, so many works have been conducted on this area, which mining from some open source dataset.

This dataset includes some basic version control system, open source hosting system and QA website, such as github, svn, jira, bugzilla, and stackoverflow.

Web Development

I have joined maintenance and developement works of some school official websites in Hubei University. Before 2016, I have some internship experiences as a web front developer in Wuhan and Beijing.
